1.1.1.0

Release Date

September 20 2014This version uses the Arnold 4.2.1.2 core.## HIGHLIGHTED NEW FEATURES

  • EXR overscan support.
  • Improved sampling (textured quad lights, Cook-Torrance specular and refraction).
  • Reduced IO for shadow ray and rough glossy texture lookups.
  • A new light manager .
  • New Volume primitive, with OpenVDB support.

HIGHLIGHTED IMPROVEMENTS

  • Volume collector to support the new volume primitive.

NOTES

  • FumeFX extension is now handled by Sitni Sati and can be downloaded through their support page.

INCOMPATIBLE CHANGES

  • Difference in microfacet BTDFs: Rough refraction viewed at grazing angles or using Phong-smoothed normals renders slightly darker now, especially for high roughness values. This affects the standard shader and AiMicrofacetBTDFIntegrate(), and fixes a bug in the previous BTDF sampling.
